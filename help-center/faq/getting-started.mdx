## Quick steps

Follow these steps to make your first project:

1. Log into your hell4uk account.
2. Click the **New Project** button on the dashboard.
3. Enter a name and optional description for your project.
4. Click **Create**.
5. Start adding docs and organizing workspaces!

## Tip

You can always add more members later from the project settings.
