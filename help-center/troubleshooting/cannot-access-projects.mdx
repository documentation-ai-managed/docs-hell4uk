---
title: Cannot access my projects
description: Troubleshoot access issues in hell4uk.
---

## Symptoms
- Project list is empty
- Access denied error

## Common Causes
- Not logged in
- Insufficient permissions

## Quick Fix
<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in again">
    ```
    Visit https://hell4uk.com/login and re-enter your credentials.
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check team access">
    ```
    Ask your project admin to confirm you have access permissions.
    ```
  </Step>
</Steps>
